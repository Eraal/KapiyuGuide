What is KapiyuGuide?

KapiyuGuide is a campus-wide inquiry management system designed to streamline communication between students and the school offices. No more long queues or confusion—allowing students to send inquiries directly to the appropriate office, receive real-time updates, and stay informed through announcements—all in one centralized platform.

How does KapiyuGuide works?

Real-Time Inquiry Responses

Stay informed with instant updates on your inquiries' status as they are processed.

Office-specific Responses

Get responses tailored to your inquiry from the specific office you've contacted, ensuring quick and accurate support.

Secure Counseling

Request confidential counseling or schedule a secure video session with offices like Guidance and Counseling or GAD.

Announcements

Catch up on important news, updates, and announcements from various university units.

This platform connects students with various campus units and offices for faster, more organized communication. Whether it's academic records, counseling, health, or student services — send your inquiry directly to the appropriate unit or office.
